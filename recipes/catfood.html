Food For My Cat

Ingredients:

  • 1 can of Hills Prescription Diet c/d for Urinary Care
  • 1 reusable sillicon can lid
  • 1 bowl, preferably with a low rim, as befits His Majesty

Steps:

  1. Open the can
  2. Scoop 1/2 of the can into the bowl
  3. Seal the can with the lid, then refrigerate leftovers
  4. Serve the bowl on a raised surface, for privacy from dogs

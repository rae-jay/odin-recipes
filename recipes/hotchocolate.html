Hot Chocolate

Ingredients:

  • 1 large spoonful of sweetened condensed milk
  • 1 slightly less large spoonful of unsweetened cocoa powder
  • Some amount of milk

Steps:

  1. Add the condensed milk to a glass
  2. Stir in the cocoa powder until mostly combined
  3. Add milk until the glass is mostly full
  4. Microwave until it burns your mouth just like... a little bit
